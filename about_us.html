Our Story So Far

It started from the campus of Delhi University where a socially aware group of students used to teach, play, laugh and spend time with underprivileged kids receiving overwhelming joy, satisfaction and peace. After spending time with them we realised how pure their emotions are and to show their gratitude and love they used to give us chocolates and candy from their own money. First step towards taking this initiative started with Christmas celebrations with these kids along with my friends.The LOVE, CARE and the PAIN they faced left us with a thought which is now "Khushiyo Ki Umang".


On 14 January 2022 our NGO completed one year with two academies and have made a powerful and supportive team of 50+ active volunteers and with their co-operation and support we have successfully organised various education, food, clothing, plantation drives. We have been teaching more than 100 childrens and have impacted their lives in a positive manner .
