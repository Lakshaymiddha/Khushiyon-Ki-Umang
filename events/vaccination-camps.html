Volunteering in Vaccination Camp

Doctors, Teachers, Army officials and volunteers have helped us survive the pandemic. And as the responsible citizens, it's our duty to help our nation in tough times. On the occasion of Independence Day our members assisted various organisations during vaccination drives.

Wherein they helped people and doctors in management and help people to register for vaccination.Finding and booking slot for people was quite difficult so volunteers helped needy people to book it.

Instagram Live Session

With so many rumours and false information regarding authenticity of vaccines people were afraid to get vaccinated to answer all the questions related to covid/vaccination/safety Instagram Live session with two doctors was organised and we posted it on our Instagram handle for future reference. People living in slums are still afraid about getting vaccinated so our aim is to motivate them to get vaccinated soon.
