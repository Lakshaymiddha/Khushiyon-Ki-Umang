Extra-Curricular Activities

India is well known for its diversity and festivals.

We celebrate all the festivals and events with utmost enthusiasm teach them the importance of festivals and reason for celebration in the form of Nukad Natak , stories, poem, drawing, dance etc. It started with Christmas celebration in 2020 when our team visited slum areas and distributed food and organised various activities such as dance and singing competitions along with playing games with them.

The joy and happiness on their innocent faces motivated us to visit them frequently. Diya making and decoration activity during Diwali made this occasion special for them. By the end of 2021 we celebrated Christmas with an expanded family and more resources, every student of NGO was given gifts and sweets.

Festivals at KKU

On Republic Day and Independence Day volunteers were dressed as freedom fighters and represented different religions to show beauty in the diversity of India. On the occasion of Raksha Bandhan students were explained why we celebrate it and they enjoy tying Rakhi to each other and we promised them to protect them from Hunger, Lack of awareness and will do every possible effort to add value in their lives.

Live performance of a singer was organised where members and students enjoyed it. Children love cartoons and animated characters so to celebrate special members dressed up as Doraemon, Angry Birds etc. We have celebrated Christmas, New Year, Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, Lohri, Republic Day, Eid, Independence day etc with members and students.Working with the tagline of #spreadTheKhushiyanYouGot along with spreading awareness about their rights, No Child Marriage Awareness Campaign was conducted to inform them about their rights and responsibility.

With positivity and hopes to continue this legacy of celebration we invite everyone to celebrate with us.
