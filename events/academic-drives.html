Academic Drives

“Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.’’ ~ Malcom X


We are organising academic drives every weekend from the past year in two academies. And providing opportunity for the youth to teach less privileged children and develop their teaching, leadership, and management skills while creating impact by imparting education and training to students who wish to learn but lack resources.

We have been teaching basic hygiene, moral values, language, maths and many more. We have grouped the students in three classes according to their prior knowledge in the subjects. Creating awareness about the importance of education is a challenge and we are overcoming it day by day and positive feedbacks received from parents is our reward.

Students have been divided in three classes on the basis of knowledge and syllabus they have covered.Assignments and tests are taken frequently in order to check if they have understood the topic completely.


We believe that quality over quantity and more focus is given towards learning rather than covering topics/syllabus.Teachers from renowned schools have helped us design syllabus.

All the stationary such as Books, Notebook, Pencil, Colours etc are provided by the NGO. Visualisation and pictorial representation create more effect and it makes education easy and fun so use of objects and pictures is done to make things more interesting and joyful.Students are rewarded with gifts and recently medals have been provided to appreciate their hard work and to motivate them to focus more on studies.


Along with academic drive, a new initiative of organising Old Age Home visit, Orphanage, Blanket Distribution,Food Distribution, Women safety Awareness and Plantation drive will also be conducted frequently.
