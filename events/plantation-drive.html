Plantation Drive

‘’You can't have fruits without the roots’’, with the climate change and harm done to nature by humans we are increasing problems for future generation because resources are scarce and future belongs to those who work for it today. Keeping in mind our responsibility towards environment we came up with the initiative of ‘’ONE UMANG ONE PLANT’’, with every new member joining in, we plan to plant a tree for them.

2 successful Plantation drives were organised in collaboration with other NGOs wherein we planted more than 100+ plants with mass participation from both the NGOs and more than 40 volunteers were involved. Our responsibility doesn’t end with just planting but it's important to help and nourish them to grow.

Plants require love and care. So places of plantation were chosen in a way that they are given proper care and resources. In future we wish to plant as many trees as we can. Infact the place where we teach them is kind of junkyard. That's why a cleanliness drive was organised where volunteers and students were divided in two teams and they cleaned the academy. The motive was to teach them the importance of hygiene and cleanliness and inculcate this habit.
